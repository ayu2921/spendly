Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you log expenses, we store the details you enter — amount, category, date, and any description you provide.

We do not collect payment information, location data, or any information beyond what you explicitly enter into the app.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Spendly service — displaying your expense history, generating summaries, and enabling filters. We do not use your data for advertising or sell it to any third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secured servers. We take reasonable precautions to protect it from unauthorized access, but no system is completely secure. You are responsible for keeping your account credentials confidential.

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly uses Google Fonts to load typefaces. This may result in your browser making a request to Google's servers. We do not integrate with any other third-party services that receive your personal or expense data.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out at privacy@spendly.app. We aim to respond within 5 business days.
